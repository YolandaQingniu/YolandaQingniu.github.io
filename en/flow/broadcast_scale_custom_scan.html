Broadcast Scale - Custom Bluetooth Scan

Broadcast scale-Custom Bluetooth Scan

This chapter is for users who have a certain understanding of Bluetooth and have their own Bluetooth framework in the app.

When the broadcast scale is measured, it will continuously emit Bluetooth broadcast signals, which will contain the information weighed by the user.

The instructions below are based on the fact that developers already have some knowledge of Bluetooth scanning. If you encounter a lot of problems, it is still recommended to use the SDK for scanning.

The workflow of this device is approximately as follows:

I. Initialization

II, scanning

Call Bluetooth scan yourself

After calling the Bluetooth scan, pass the scanned broadcast information to the SDK via QNBleApi.buildBroadcastDevice

Results Processing
